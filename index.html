This site is part of the project “The Trip” crea­ted at the Cologne Design Academy (Kölner Design Aka­demie) in the course “popup and Merchandising”. 4th Semester. The project was under the direction of Nina Nowacki & Natasha Cvetkovic.

“The Trip” is a pop-up book created by Lorena Salome Thiel, Jessica Bensch, Angelika Würz and Thibault Jan Beyer. The book is about a teenager's first experience with LSD. Threw built-in effects the reader gets a deep insight of this drug. This website was created in the same spirit as the book with the aim to give the visitor an audiovisual expe­rience and be an appetizer for the book. Have fun!
